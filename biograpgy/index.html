Significant People

Here you will read about some of the most important people in ISRO

Vikram Ambalal Sarabhai

Tenure: 1963-1972

He was an astronomer and physicist from India who started space exploration and contributed to the country's development of nuclear power. He conceived of the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), formerly known as the Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR), established by the Indian government in 1962.

Read More

Mr. Satish Dhawan

Tenure: 1972-1984

He was an Indian mathematician and aerospace engineer, widely regarded as the father of experimental fluid dynamics research in India. was one of the most eminent researchers in the field of turbulence and boundary layers, leading the successful and indigenous development of the Indian space programme. He is also known for being ISRO's chairman for the longest tenure (12 years).

Read More

Mr. S. Somanath

Tenure: 2018-2022

He is the current Chairman of the ISRO. He currently serves as the Secretary (Space) and ex-officio chairman of both the Space Commission and the Indian Space Research Organisation.Under his watch, 15 successful satellite missions were accomplished with the propulsion systems supplied from LPSC (ISRO’s Liquid Propulsion Systems Centre).

Read More

Dr. P K Mishra

Tenure: 2019-Current

He is the 13th and current principal secretary to the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi. He has held a number of important positions for the governments of Gujarat and India, including Additional Chief Secretary (Revenue), Principal Secretary (Agriculture and Cooperation), Special Secretary in the Ministry of Home Affairs, Additional Secretary in the Ministry of Urban Development, and Member Secretary of National Capital Region Planning Board.

Mr. Ajit Doval

Tenure: 2019-Current

He is the National Security Advisor (NSA) of India to Prime Minister Narendra Modi. He was Intelligence Bureau (IB) chief and Indian Police Service (IPS) officer from Kerala cadre.He was the founder director of the right of centre leaning think tank Vivekananda International Foundation (VIF), before appointment as NSA.

Read More

Mr. Rajiv Gauba

Tenure: 2019-Current

He is an Indian civil servant serving as the current Cabinet Secretary of India since 2019. He is an Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer belonging to the 1982 batch of Jharkhand (earlier Bihar) cadre. Prior to his appointment as Cabinet Secretary, i.e., the head of civil services in India, he served with distinction as the Union Home Secretary, Union Urban Development Secretary, and the Chief Secretary of Jharkhand.

Mr. T.V. Somanathan

Tenure: 2021-Current

He is presently the Finance Secretary of India (Department of expenditure).He has served in various positions for both the Government of India and the Government of Tamil Nadu, like Secretary to Chief Minister, Managing Director of Chennai Metro Rail Corporation Ltd, Joint Secretary in Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

Mr. Vinay Mohan Kwatra

Tenure: 2022-Current

He is an Indian diplomat of IFS cadre serving as the 34th and the current Foreign Secretary of India. Previously, he has served as the Ambassador of India to France and Nepal. He has also served at Ministry of External Affairs, India and Prime Minister's Office as Joint Secretary. He has served at different positions in many of India's missions abroad and in India. Kwatra holds an experience of nearly 32 years in a range of assignments.
